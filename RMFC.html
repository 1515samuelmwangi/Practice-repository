Real Madrid description

Real Madrid is a professional football club based in Madrid,Spain. They play at the Santiago Bernabeau Stadium.They have 32 La Liga titles and 10 UEFA Champions League titles and are widely regarded to as the best football club in the world.They are also nicknamed the Los Blancos or the Los Meringues.
